But that was a problem for me as I love to make things more complicated than they really need to be. And because of this, I ended up spending plenty more hours on the debugging process than I should have had to. Then came the debugging issue. Once my code started running, I began to see all of the little issues that had popped up in my code. Luckily, for the vast majority of issues, they were quick solves that sent me on my way fairly quick. But for some of these issues, I got caught in them like a fish in a net. These issues were things like trying to solve the reason as to why the CSS code and JavaScript weren't showing up for my index.html or my 404.html files. Long story short, they were honestly very minor issues. In fact one of them was exactly this: "if (res.url == '/index.html' || '/') {"
